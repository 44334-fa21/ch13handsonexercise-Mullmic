Three Possible Activities to Promote the Site

Activity 1

Using pay per click advertising as things like Google AdWords can make adverts around the different keywords we use.

Activity 2

Using content marketing as the new content we make can be shown off to more people gaining possibly outside interest.

Activity 3

Using email marketing as we can send updates to people that have signed up for a newsletter we have so that our business is still in the customers mind after leaving the site.
